We Have Adopted Ajitpur Village

On the sacred banks of the Ganga, Ajitpur is more than a village, it's a community ready for transformation. Through sustainable waste management, eco-awareness, and innovative practices, we are shaping a cleaner, greener future for 1,500 residents and creating a model for replication across Himalayan villages.



Your donations help us provide tools, training, and resources directly to the village, making a real difference.
