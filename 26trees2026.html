About the Initiative

To mark the beginning of 2026, HimVillageePrahari Foundation launched “26 Trees for 2026 – Challenge Us to Plant More.”

We committed to planting 26 trees on 1 January 2026. This number represents the year - not a limit. Public participation challenges us to responsibly plant more while strengthening our overall environmental mission.

26 trees is our starting commitment - not the limit.
Every contribution strengthens long-term environmental action.

Core Objectives

  • Plant a minimum of 26 native trees
  • Enable global New Year environmental participation
  • Support plantation, waste management & awareness programs
  • Build early momentum for 2026

Global Participation

  1. Plant trees locally
  2. Click a photo or short video
  3. Post in this year 2026
  4. Tag HimVillageePrahari Foundation

#26TreesFor2026 · #26in2026 · #NewYearGreenResolution

What Happened on 1 January 2026

  • 11 saplings planted with community participation
  • 24 saplings preserved for upcoming plantation
  • 15 saplings planned under phased plantation

We prioritise survival, care, and long-term impact over one-day numbers.
