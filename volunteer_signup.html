Why Volunteer with Him Village ePrahari?

At Him Village ePrahari, we believe in the power of community to solve global challenges. Our volunteers are the backbone of our mission to combat pollution, promote sustainable living, and preserve the environment for future generations. Whether you’re passionate about nature, education, or social change, there’s a role for you. Together, we can create a lasting impact!

  • Contribute to community-based environmental initiatives.
  • Work on local and national projects to tackle pollution.
  • Gain hands-on experience in project management and sustainability efforts.
  • Network with like-minded individuals who share your passion for change.
  • Receive a Certificate of Volunteering and recommendation letters for exceptional work.

How You Can Help?

Volunteers play an integral part in every project we undertake. Some of your tasks may include:

  • Participate in environmental campaigns.
  • Organize and execute tree planting and cleanup events.
  • Help in organizing educational workshops and webinars.
  • Support in administrative tasks, content creation, and fundraising activities.
  • Share your innovative ideas to improve our environmental initiatives.
